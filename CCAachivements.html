My CCA achivements

My CCA for the past few years in secondary school is NPCC.

In NPCC I participated actively in all activities and earned myself many badges and the rank of staff sergeant.

One of the most notable badge I've earned is the marksmanship badge. To get this badge I had to go to HTA and attain a high score in the live firing test. Only five people in the whole batch got it

In my CCA I also had the exco position of Head Of Logistics. I handled the logistics of everything in my CCA. For example if we were doing rifle drills that day i would be the one to prepare the rifles.
